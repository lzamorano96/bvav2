Assumptions & methodology

Net revenue = (Σ units × price across your sales tiers) − refunds, applied at one overall refund rate. Paying Customers = total units − refunds. Partner payout applies a marginal (progressive) rev-share to net revenue — each revenue band earns its own rate (25% to $50k, 30% to $100k, 40% above). These rev-share bands are separate from your sales tiers. The blended rate is the weighted average across those bands (payout ÷ net revenue). Marketing Value sums only the enabled marketing channels. Launch Email, PDP Video and Affiliate Network are revenue-scaled — valued as a share of net revenue and clamped to typical ranges ($15K–$75K, $5K–$25K and $10K–$50K respectively); these are directional upside, not guarantees. Credit cost declines geometrically to the month-12 target.

Methodology & rates are estimates for illustration purposes only. Not a guarantee of performance or payout. v0.1.0 · BVA
