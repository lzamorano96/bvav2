    Assumptions & methodology

    Net revenue = units × price − refunds. Partner payout applies a marginal (progressive) stepped rev-share to net revenue — each band of revenue earns its own rate (e.g. the first $50k at 25%, the next $50k at 30%, anything above $100k at 40%). The blended rate shown on the Partner Payout card is the weighted average across those bands (payout ÷ net revenue), so it sits between the tier rates. Marketing value sums six channel valuations at the rates shown in Assumptions. Credit cost declines geometrically to the month-12 target.

    Methodology & rates are estimates for illustration purposes only. Not a guarantee of performance or payout. v0.1.0 · BVA
